The next two videos give you a roadmap for navigating the course. We will introduce you to the different video series we've created for you.

  • Core content : where we introduce new concepts and techniques.

  • Concept challenges : we invite you to work through difficult questions and learn from others.

  • Additional support : we work through more detailed examples and explanations for further practice.

  • In the Real World : Google engineers share how they use the key Computer Science tools we talk about.

  • When I Struggled : we share stories of the learning journeys of the instructors and fellow students.

  • Project : we give overview and tips for the programming assignments.

** Note for learners working on mobile devices: many of our videos use In Video Questions. These are not currently supported on the Coursera mobile app, so we recommend you watch our videos on your computer as much as possible to get the complete experience of this course. **

If you're familiar with our course structure from Course 1 in our Specialization, feel free to skip ahead to the project description and jump right in developing your project.
